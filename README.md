# Business-Analyst-Salary-in-India-2024-Fresher-to-Experienced
The average Business Analyst Salary ranges between ₹ 2.7 Lakhs to ₹ 11 Lakhs in India. However, with a substantial increase in years of experience, the Business Analyst Salary can go up to INR 15 LPA. Some top-paying companies in India for Business Analyst are Amazon, JP Morgan Chase, and HSBC Group.
